Introduction

We use different words to talk about our daily activities:

  • Morning activities: wake up, get up, brush teeth, have breakfast, get dressed
  • School activities: study, read, write, listen, learn
  • Afternoon activities: play sports, do homework, watch TV, use the computer
  • Evening activities: have dinner, take a shower, go to bed

We can talk about when we do these activities:

  • in the morning / afternoon / evening
  • before / after school
  • at 7 o'clock / at night
