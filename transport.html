Introduction

We use different types of transport to travel from one place to another:

  • Road transport: car, bus, bicycle, motorbike, taxi, van, truck
  • Rail transport: train, underground/subway, tram
  • Air transport: airplane, helicopter
  • Water transport: boat, ship, ferry

We also use different travel verbs with transport:

  • drive a car
  • ride a bicycle/motorbike
  • catch/take a bus/train
  • fly in an airplane
  • sail on a boat
