Introduction

We use different verb forms to talk about when things happen:

  • Present simple: I play football every day.
  • Present continuous: I am playing football now.
  • Past simple: I played football yesterday.
  • Going to future: I am going to play football tomorrow.

We also use different verb patterns:

  • Verb + infinitive: I want to play.
  • Verb + -ing: I enjoy playing.
  • Modal verbs: I can play, I must play.
