Introduction

We use time expressions to say when things happen:

  • Days of the week: on Monday, on Tuesday...
  • Parts of the day: in the morning, in the afternoon, at night
  • Specific times: at 3 o'clock, at half past four
  • Time clauses: when I get home, after school, before dinner

Time expressions help us talk about when we do different activities!
