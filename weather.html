Introduction

We talk about the weather every day! The weather can be:

  • Sunny - when the sun is shining
  • Cloudy - when there are clouds in the sky
  • Rainy - when rain falls from the sky
  • Windy - when the wind blows strongly
  • Snowy - when snow falls from the sky

There are four seasons in a year:

  • Spring - flowers grow and baby animals are born
  • Summer - hot and sunny days
  • Autumn (or Fall) - leaves change color and fall from trees
  • Winter - cold days, sometimes with snow
