Introduction

Animals are all around us! Some animals live with us as pets:

  • Dogs, cats, rabbits, birds, and fish

Some animals live on farms:

  • Horses, cows, goats, sheep, and chickens

And some are wild animals that live in nature:

  • Elephants, giraffes, lions, monkeys, and crocodiles
