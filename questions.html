Introduction

We use question words to ask for information:

  • What - to ask about things: What is your favorite color?
  • Where - to ask about places: Where do you live?
  • When - to ask about time: When is your birthday?
  • Why - to ask about reasons: Why are you sad?
  • Who - to ask about people: Who is your best friend?
  • How - to ask about manner: How do you make a cake?

We also use yes/no questions that start with verbs:

  • Do/Does/Did - Do you like ice cream?
  • Is/Are/Was/Were - Is it raining?
  • Can/Could - Can you swim?
