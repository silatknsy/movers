Introduction

We use different words to talk about food and meals:

  • Meals: breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack
  • Food groups: fruit, vegetables, meat, fish, dairy, grains
  • Drinks: water, milk, juice, tea
  • Places to eat: home, restaurant, caf√©, school canteen

We can talk about food preferences:

  • I like / I don't like
  • My favorite food is...
  • I prefer... to...
